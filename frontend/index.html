Our mission

Better outcomes for students who learn differently

Standard exam prep wasn't built for everyone. Students with dyslexia, ADHD, and other learning differences often face barriers that have nothing to do with how smart they are — slow feedback, inaccessible practice conditions, and tools that ignore how they actually learn.

Paramark exists to change that. One of our founders grew up struggling with dyslexia, and knows first-hand how much difference instant feedback and the right exam conditions can make. We're building the tools we wish we'd had.

Around 6.3 million people in the UK have dyslexia, making up roughly 10% of the population. Of these, about 4% (over 2 million) experience severe dyslexia.
